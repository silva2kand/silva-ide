# /silva-system-operator:new-lead

Create a new lead in the pipeline with evidence and next actions.

## Inputs
- Lead type (business/property/premises/professional)
- Link(s) or name + location
- Budget/price (if known)

## Steps
1. Capture the exact URL(s) and discovery timestamp.
2. Pull the key facts (numbers, location, contacts, constraints).
3. Do a quick plausibility check and list missing information.
4. Draft:
   - 1 short outreach email
   - 1 detailed email
   - 1 follow-up
5. Stop for approval before sending.
